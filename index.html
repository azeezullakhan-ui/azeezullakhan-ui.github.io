Risk & Return Analysis of Selected Pharmaceutical Companies Listed on BSE
Guide: Prof. R. Mahesh, Chairman, B.N. BIMS  ·  Jan – Apr 2025
  • Analysed 24 months of stock price data (2023–2024) for 15 BSE-listed pharma companies using Beta and CAGR in MS Excel; identified Aurobindo Pharma (CAGR: 80.78%) and Lupin (CAGR: 78.48%) as top performers

  • Performed current and quick ratio analysis across 3 financial years for all 15 companies; concluded Abbott India and IPCA Laboratories as most suitable for risk-averse investors based on low Beta and consistent liquidity
  • Submitted research report to University of Mysore in partial fulfilment of IV Semester MBA degree; presented findings to faculty panel in structured format
